Description of our task:

1) We recognize the need to develop an aspect-based sentiment analysis system for customer reviews.
2) This system would leverage NLP techniques to extract specific aspects mentioned in reviews and determine the sentiment associated with each aspect.
3) By analyzing customer reviews at an aspect level, the system can provide valuable insights into various aspects of a product or service that customers appreciate or have concerns about.
4) This information can guide businesses in making informed decisions for improving their offerings and enhancing customer satisfaction.
5) We combine the extracted aspects and their associated sentiment to form aspect-sentiment pairs and visualize the aspect-sentiment pairs to gain insights into customer opinions.
6) We deployed it as an application or API, allowing users to input customer reviews and obtain sentiment analysis results for different aspects.
